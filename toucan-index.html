Two pathways, one mission

Toucan Education is a volunteer-run nonprofit that provides free academic and artistic enrichment for middle schoolers. Students can pick math, music, or both.

AMC 8 Math Lab

Weekly workshops covering number sense, algebra, geometry, and creative problem solving. Includes mock contests and feedback.
